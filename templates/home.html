About

Who we are

HAPPY US is creating awareness about mental health and Spreading happiness. From staying indoors to ensure safety to recurrent upsurges in corona cases, losses both family and financial has buckled our head with plenty of stress and anxiety. Looking at overwhelming concerns, Team Happy us felt a need to share people's plight.

There is no Guarantee of the world being a better place in the near future, but our team sure tries to make it happier. We tackle a major problem regarding mental health: people keeping their feelings to themselves in the fear of being ignored, or even worse, made fun of. Our app allows users to vent to us, whilst staying anonymous. Our team doesn't find out who the user is, but tries to help in any way possible. Our guarantee? Vent out to us, because this vent does not go to other rooms.

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is Happy Us?

    Happy Us is an application concerning mental health. This app lets you vent out to us, so that you don't have to keep your feelings to yourself anymore.

  • Where is it available?

    Download the app either from play store or app store and you can also contact us through our website and Socials.

  • How does the app work?

    This app lets you share your problems to us, while staying anonymous. Open the app and vent out to your heart's content, so that you stay content.

  • Why "Happy Us"?

    We believe that a happy world starts with one happy person. In hope that the world sees happiness, this app is called "Happy Us".
